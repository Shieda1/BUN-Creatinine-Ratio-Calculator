What is the BUN creatinine ratio calculator?
BUN creatinine ratio calculator is a tool that measures the ratio of a patient's blood urea nitrogen to their creatinine levels.
How to calculate BUN creatinine ratio?
To calculate BUN creatinine ratio you should use the following formula:
BUN creatinine ratio = BUN blood urea nitrogen / serum creatinine
